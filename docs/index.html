The Studio

Small team. Wild imagination.

Wild Olive Studios was founded on a simple, slightly stubborn idea: that the most memorable games come from people who care more about the feeling of a world than the size of its budget.

We're a tiny independent team. We write our own code, draw our own art, and compose our own quiet little soundtracks. Like an olive tree, we grow slowly, we grow gnarled and characterful, and what we make is meant to last.

This site grows alongside the studio — as we ship new games, they'll take their place in the grove above.
