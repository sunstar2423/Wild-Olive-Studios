Build the city's network

Hit each milestone to grow your reputation. Clear them all to become the city's official Transit Authority.

How to play

1. Trace a line. Choose Bus, Rail or Ferry under the map, then click stops in order. Ferries only link waterside stops. Hit Build line.

Region & towns. The map is a whole region of towns. Zoom out (scroll / pinch / －) to see them all and the intercity links; tap a town to dive into its streets. Zoom in to a town to place local stops; zoom out to plan the network. Each town has its own randomised set of places to connect.

Get around. Scroll/pinch to zoom (＋ / －), drag to pan, and in close-up tap any station to look inside — the place, the queue, and your vehicles pulling in. Esc / Back to return.

2. Carry passengers. People appear at stops wanting to reach another place — within a town, or to another town entirely. They board, transfer at interchange stops, and pay a fare on arrival — longer trips, ferries and intercity pay more.

🚄 Go intercity. Pick the Intercity tool and click two or more towns to link their central stations with a fast, high-capacity line. Connect a town's central station to its local stops so travellers can transfer all the way through.

3. Grow & maintain. Add vehicles to busy lines, open new routes, and Service vehicles before wear gets high — neglected vehicles break down, costing money and happiness. Short on cash? The 🏦 Bank (Lines tab) lets you borrow to expand and repay as fares roll in — interest accrues daily, so clear debt when you can.

4. Watch the numbers. Each line card shows its riders and fares today — spot the earners and the duds. Toggle 🔥 Demand to see journeys people want but can't make. Set your 🎟️ Fare level (Lines tab): low fares grow ridership and goodwill, premium fares earn more per trip. The nightly report can be set to auto-continue if you'd rather keep rolling.

🎯 Chase the goals. The banner on the map shows your next milestone — each one pays a city grant (up to £30k) that funds your next expansion. Clear them all and grow your net worth to win.

5. Weather & event days. The top bar shows today's conditions. Event days — Match Day at the Stadium, Opera Night, the Arts Festival, Market Day and more — surge demand at specific venues, so pre-position vehicles to cash in. Weather sways outdoor demand (sunny Beach Days are golden), and rough seas slow ferries and wear them faster — keep them serviced through storms.

6. Saving & sound. Your city auto-saves every night. Tap 💾 (or S) to save, 🔊 (or M) for sound, and pick Continue on the title screen to return. Shortcuts: 1/2/3 modes, H demand, Space pause.

Tidewater Lines

The coastal city of Olivehaven is growing, and its people need a way to move. You've been handed the keys to a brand-new clean-transit authority and a modest budget.

Stitch the city together with electric buses, sleek light rail, and breezy ferries — then zoom out and grow across a whole region of towns, linking them with fast intercity lines. Carry real passengers near and far, collect fares, reinvest, and build the network this coast deserves.

  • 🗺️ A whole region to grow into — your home town plus more towns, each with its own randomised streets and places.
  • 🚄 Connect town to town with intercity rail and ferries; passengers travel right across the map.
  • 👥 Watch live passengers board, transfer at interchanges, and ride to their stops — zoom in to step inside any station.
  • 🏟️ Ride the rhythm of the region — Match Days, festivals and weather shift demand; rough seas slow the ferries.
  • 💷 Balance profit & happiness — expand smartly, maintain your fleet, borrow when you must, and keep riders smiling.
