Build the city's network

Hit each milestone to grow your reputation. Clear them all to become the city's official Transit Authority.

How to play

1. Trace a line. Choose Bus, Rail or Ferry under the map, then click stops in order. Ferries only link waterside stops. Hit Build line.

2. Carry passengers. People appear at stops wanting to reach another place. They board your vehicles, transfer at shared interchange stops, and pay a fare on arrival — longer trips and ferries pay more.

3. Grow & maintain. Add vehicles to busy lines, open new routes, and Service vehicles before wear gets high — neglected vehicles break down, costing money and happiness.

4. Watch the numbers. Toggle 🔥 Demand to see where people want to travel but can't yet. Each night you get a profit/loss report. Keep funds above zero and riders happy.

5. Saving. Your city auto-saves every night. Tap 💾 (or press S) to save any time, and pick Continue on the title screen to return to it. Shortcuts: 1/2/3 pick a mode, H toggles demand, Space pauses.

Tidewater Lines

The coastal city of Olivehaven is growing, and its people need a way to move. You've been handed the keys to a brand-new clean-transit authority and a modest budget.

Stitch the city together with electric buses, sleek light rail, and breezy ferries. Carry real passengers from the university to the beach, from the business district to the opera house. Collect fares, reinvest, and build the network this bay deserves.

  • 🚌 Trace routes between 18 unique destinations — libraries, an opera house, beaches, a forest park, the harbour and more.
  • 👥 Watch live passengers board, transfer at interchanges, and ride to their stops.
  • 💷 Balance profit & happiness — expand smartly, maintain your fleet, and keep riders smiling.
