Groove Grove

Drum machine · beat lab · rhythm school

Welcome to your studio. Every sound here is synthesized live — you're not playing recordings, you're playing instruments.

🎛 Studio

  • Program beats — tap cells in the 16-step grid. Columns are 16th-notes; the numbers mark beats 1·2·3·4.
  • Layer music — switch to the Bass and Keys tabs to add basslines and chord stabs on top of your drums. Everything is tuned to one scale, so it always sounds good together.
  • Be the drummer — smash the live pads (keys A–K). Arm ● Rec while the beat plays and your hits are quantized straight into the grid, just like a real drum machine.
  • Build songs — make patterns A–D, chain them (+A +B…), then hit Song mode.
  • Shape the groove — tempo, swing for shuffle feel, four drum kits, and a full mixer with mute/solo.

🎓 Beat School

  • 12 levels teach real grooves — four-on-the-floor, boom bap, dembow, the clave, trap, funk…
  • Listen to the target, rebuild it on the grid, then check your beat to earn ★★★.
  • Stars unlock the Neon (8★) and Junkyard (16★) kits.

⌨ Shortcuts

Space play/stop · A–K drum pads · 1–4 patterns
