❓ How to Play GreenAcres

Goal (Campaign): Reach Sustainability ≥ 75 AND $5,000 by end of Year 2 (week 96).

Core Loop: Click a tile → Till it → Select a crop in Seed Shop → Plant → Advance Week to grow → Harvest when ready → Sell in Inventory → Buy more seeds!

Soil & Water: Watch soil health and moisture. Irrigate dry tiles. Rotate crops to avoid monoculture penalty. Legumes (beans, soybean) restore soil.

Sustainability: Use organic fertiliser, grow diverse crops, buy green tech. Higher sustainability unlocks premium prices and calmer weather.

Tech: Buy upgrades in the Tech tab. Greenhouse lets you plant in winter; Drip Irrigation halves water costs; Compost System makes organic free.

Market: Prices fluctuate weekly. Selling too much of one crop drops its price. Watch for demand events (bonus prices in Market tab).

Seasons: Spring/Summer/Autumn/Winter — 12 weeks each. Crops grow best in their season. Nothing grows outdoors in winter.
