Emberkeep

The supply boat will not come again. Snow has sealed the channel and the winter has no end that you can see. But the light must turn — it has always turned — and a light needs fire.

You are the keeper. When the driftwood runs out, there is only one thing left to burn: what you remember. Trade memories for fire, and survive the dark.

  • 🚢 Tend the light. Place mirrors to bend the beam and guide lost ships home — each one brings driftwood for the fire.
  • 🔥 Feed the fire. When the cold outpaces the wood, burn a memory to keep warm. It will warm you. It will cost you.
  • 🤍 Keep who you are. Survive all eight nights to the thaw. What you choose to save decides who greets the spring.

Tap an empty cell to place a mirror, tap again to flip it ◤ / ◢, once more to take it back. Headphones recommended.
