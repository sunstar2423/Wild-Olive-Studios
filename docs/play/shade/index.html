Shade

a tiny hollow-knight-like platformer

Paper Lantern Studios

How to Play

  • Move with ← → or A D.
  • Jump with Space / Z / J. Hold for a higher leap.
  • Attack with X / K. Time strikes to defeat shades.
  • Collect light shards and find the lantern door to escape each cave.
  • Find the Moth Wings power-up to gain a double jump.

Tip: press R to restart a level, Esc for the menu.
